About FRIDAY

FRIDAY is a song I wrote the lyrics for while recording it. It didn’t make much sense at the time, but it’s taken its own shape now — and I like it.

For the beat, I sampled an old song I made back when I was living in Beijing during COVID. I was renting a room in an apartment on the 20th floor of a building, with a huge circular window. My desk was right in the middle of it, and I could see far — but there was nothing to look at except the emptiness of a city of twenty million people under lockdown.

Written by Malïao & produced by HOMEMAD. PROD · Recorded between Beijing & Paris · 2018/2025
